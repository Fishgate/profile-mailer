D&AD IN BOOK

This year Fishgate was awarded with a prestigious D&AD In Book award in the category Graphic Design, Posters - for the World of Birds campaign.

The innovative campaign for this Hout Bay-based bird sanctuary, did much to promote the sanctuary by increasing ticket sales substantially during the winter months - traditionally their quietest time.
 

BOSAL

We secured the "re-fresh" of two Bosal digital entities namely: Bosal.co.za and the Bosal Blog - to ensure user-friendly navigation, updated content and the incorporation of optimised design elements. Work commenced this November and will continue into 2014.
 

OIM

Fishgate was appointed to design and build a brand new website for the OIM group - which specialises in business performance improvement with a specific focus on people management, organisational performance and operational optimisation.

CTRACK

In 2013, Fishgate developed a new campaign for Ctrack - continuing the "Always Visible" theme that has become synonymous with the brand.

The campaign included Billboards, Car stickers, Licence discs, Print advertisements and Digital elements - tapping into the "My Family" sticker trend to convey that Ctrack's tracking systems can keep your family, Always Visible - regardless of whether it is your human or Fleet family.

Watch out for the Titans "cricket family" that was created as Ctrack proudly became one of the team's co-sponsors this year.
